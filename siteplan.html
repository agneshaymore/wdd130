Site Name

Cheeri-Oars Rafting Company

Site Purpose

To describe the location, level of challenge, equipment available, fees, staff experience, and nearby restaurants and lodgings.

Target Audience

Family groups with children above minimum height limit; Teens must be accompanied by an adult; Adults with novice to moderate experience; Dog owners must outfit their dogs with their own appropriate canine life vest.

Logo

Top view of raft with oars.

Color Scheme

Colors of northern California: golden yellow, tree greens, water blues, wood browns for some text.

Typography

Open Sans Serif for text, Merriweather Sans for headings.
