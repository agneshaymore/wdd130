Hikes

John Nicholas trail at Sanborn County Park

Mountain biking trails. There is a flat/easy section at the beginning and then you can continue up the more steep and challenging section which takes you to the Ridge Trail on top of the hill.

Almaden Quicksilver County Park

Some nice trails for hiking or biking. There is some mining equipment/information on display.

Calero County Park

A favorite place to bike. Try the Lisa Killough trail and Cottle Trail loop.

Goat Rock Overlook Loop at Castle Rock State Park

A favorite hike about 3-4 miles and it has views of the Santa Cruz mountains and a waterfall if youâ€™re lucky.

Golden Gate National Recreation Area

Definitely worth a visit. Great views of SF and the Bay.

Uvas Canyon near Morgan Hill

A beautiful and shady place to hike.

Mount Tamalpais State Park

A lot to offer. Try the Cataract Falls hike and the Dipsea/Steep Ravine Loop. For the Dipsea trail, a hike down to Stinson Beach has a snack shack for a mid hike break.

Point Reyes National Seashore

Worth a visit.
