Mathematics

Beast Academy

The textbook is a comic book available both in paper and an online version. For the lover of math and math puzzles. Challenging. Grade k-6

Wildmathcurriculum.com

For nature-based, early gradeschool math skills.

Singapore Math

For Workbook-based Math. All grades.

Science

My Pals Science

For Workbook-based science curriculum. All grades.

Next Level Homeschool

I did the 1-week science class. A big narrated slide show with a video or two and yet she made it pretty engaging (and we had one kitchen experiment).

Social Studies

Well-trained mind's Story of the World

History is interesting with this one! It is an all-ages read aloud accessible world history narrative with age-appropriate ways to expand the curriculum. We pair it with Crash Course History videos.

Blossom and Root

They have some other good-looking science/nature books for young kids and a history curriculum focusing on minority groups.
